# Renewable Energy Certificates Platform

## Overview

A renewable energy certificate trading platform with verified green energy production tracking. The system facilitates the issuance, trading, and retirement of renewable energy certificates while ensuring transparency and preventing double-counting.

## Features

### Core Functionality
- **Certificate Issuance**: Generate RECs for verified renewable energy production
- **Trading Platform**: Peer-to-peer certificate marketplace
- **Production Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of green energy generation
- **Verification System**: Third-party validation of renewable energy claims
- **Retirement Management**: Certificate retirement for compliance and offsetting

### Smart Contract Structure

**Main Contract: `renewable-energy-certs`**

**Primary Functions:**
1. `issue-certificate` - Issue RECs for verified production
2. `trade-certificate` - Facilitate certificate trading
3. `retire-certificate` - Remove certificates from circulation
4. `verify-production` - Validate energy production claims
5. `track-green-energy` - Monitor renewable energy generation

**Data Maps:**
- Certificate registry with production metadata
- Trading marketplace and price discovery
- Producer verification and compliance tracking
- Retirement records for environmental claims
